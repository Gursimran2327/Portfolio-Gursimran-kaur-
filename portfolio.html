Featured Recipes

Palak Paneer

A classic Indian dish made with spinach and paneer (Indian cottage cheese). Palak Paneer is a nutritious and flavorful curry that combines tender spinach leaves with creamy paneer cubes, simmered in a rich tomato-based sauce infused with aromatic spices.

Specialty: Rich in vitamins, minerals, and protein, Palak Paneer is a wholesome and satisfying vegetarian dish that pairs perfectly with warm naan or steamed rice.

Dal Makhani

A creamy and indulgent lentil dish made with black lentils (urad dal) and kidney beans (rajma), slow-cooked with butter, cream, and a blend of aromatic spices. Dal Makhani is a beloved North Indian delicacy known for its rich and velvety texture, hearty flavors, and comforting aroma.

Specialty: Dal Makhani is a quintessential comfort food that is high in protein, fiber, and essential nutrients. Served hot with rice or naan, it's a soul-satisfying dish that warms the heart and nourishes the body.

Butter Chicken

A decadent and creamy chicken dish cooked in a rich tomato-based sauce with butter, cream, and a blend of aromatic spices. Butter Chicken, also known as Murgh Makhani, is a popular Indian curry celebrated for its velvety texture, bold flavors, and irresistible taste.

Specialty: Butter Chicken is a crowd-pleasing favorite that offers a perfect balance of sweet, savory, and spicy flavors. Served with naan or rice, it's a true indulgence that satisfies the senses.

Aloo Gobi

A comforting and wholesome vegetarian dish made with potatoes (aloo), cauliflower (gobi), and a blend of aromatic spices. Aloo Gobi is a simple yet flavorful curry that highlights the natural sweetness of the vegetables and the warmth of Indian spices.

Specialty: Aloo Gobi is a versatile and nutritious dish that can be enjoyed as a side dish or a main course. With its hearty flavors and satisfying texture, it's a beloved classic in Indian cuisine.

Paneer Tikka Masala

A vibrant and flavorful vegetarian curry made with marinated paneer (Indian cottage cheese) cooked in a spiced tomato-based sauce. Paneer Tikka Masala is a popular Indian dish known for its bold colors, robust flavors, and irresistible taste.

Specialty: Paneer Tikka Masala is a delightful vegetarian alternative to traditional meat curries. With its rich and creamy sauce and tender paneer cubes, it's a must-try for all Indian food enthusiasts.

Cooking Tutorials with Gursimran

Palak Paneer

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds. Let them crackle.
  2. Add finely chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook until the raw smell disappears.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn soft.
  5. Add spinach puree and cook for a few minutes.
  6. Add paneer cubes, garam masala, and salt. Mix well and simmer for 5-7 minutes.
  7. Garnish with cream and serve hot with rice or naan.

Dal Makhani

  1. Rinse and soak black lentils and kidney beans overnight.
  2. Pressure cook the soaked lentils and beans until soft.
  3. Heat ghee in a pan and add cumin seeds, chopped onions, and green chilies. Sauté until onions turn golden brown.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté until fragrant.
  5. Add chopped tomatoes, red chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Cook until tomatoes are mushy.
  6. Add cooked lentils and beans to the pan. Mix well and simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  7. Add cream and butter, and simmer for another 5 minutes. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot.

Butter Chicken

  1. Marinate chicken pieces with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Refrigerate for 2 hours.
  2. Grill or roast the marinated chicken until cooked.
  3. Heat butter in a pan and add chopped onions. Sauté until golden brown.
  4. Add tomato puree, red chili powder, garam masala, and salt. Cook until the sauce thickens.
  5. Add cooked chicken to the sauce and simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  6. Finish with cream and kasuri methi. Serve hot with rice or naan.

Aloo Gobi

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds and hing. Let them splutter.
  2. Add chopped onions and sauté until translucent.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook until raw smell disappears.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Cook until tomatoes are soft.
  5. Add cauliflower florets and potato cubes. Mix well.
  6. Cover and cook until vegetables are tender.
  7. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot with roti or rice.

Paneer Tikka Masala

  1. Marinate paneer cubes with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric powder, and garam masala. Refrigerate for 1 hour.
  2. Grill or roast the marinated paneer until golden brown.
  3. Heat oil in a pan and add chopped onions. Sauté until translucent.
  4. Add tomato puree, red chili powder, garam masala, and salt. Cook until the sauce thickens.
  5. Add grilled paneer cubes to the sauce and simmer for 5-7 minutes.
  6. Finish with cream and kasuri methi. Serve hot with naan or rice.
